Biography

Yee-Fan is a Ph.D. candidate from the School of Information Technology, Monash University Malaysia. He is interested in the application related to AI, especially for healthcare and computer-aided diagnosis. He has been actively involved in different research projects related to AI, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. His current research focuses are on biomedical signal processing, computer-aided diagnosis, and deep learning.
